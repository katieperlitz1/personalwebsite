Kesper Design

Since 2019 I've been running my own graphic design business primarily through my Instagram page, @kesperdesign. I've created over 500 designs for high school athletes committing to college, graphics for sports teams and programs, and logos and YouTube thumbnails.

How Does It Work?

Players and teams message me on Instagram page and I negotiate pricing and fulfill their orders. I do anywhere from 5 to 15 edits per week and work with top players in lacrosse, football, soccer, basketball, hockey, and more.

As a branch of Kesper Design, I have an editing page specifically for golf commitments: @kespergolfcommits. I've grown to 500 followers and completed over 50 edits since starting this page in Spring 2023, and am looking to grow it as the #1 golf recruitment page on Instagram.

Etsy

I also design and sell Canva templates on Etsy for teams to use as social media graphics. These include schedules, player spotlights, Gameday announcements, tryouts/event flyers, and other necessary promotional posts. Since starting my store in Spring 2023, I've become a top seller in the sports graphics niche and have generated over $2500 in revenue. I'm looking to expand and scale my store with more products in the next few months!
