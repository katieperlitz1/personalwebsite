Hi! I'm Katie

I'm a designer, entrepreneur, and Information Science, Systems, and Technology student at Cornell University

Who Am I?

I'm endlessly curious, fueled by early-morning cold brew and business ideas, and obsessed with building things that feel both useful and fun. I work best at the intersection of logic and creativity. I learn fast, ask a lot of questions, and get most excited when something complex finally clicks.

Outside of work, I'm probably at the gym, editing a sports graphic in Photoshop, or listening to podcasts on tech, design, or entrepreneurship.

More About Me

What do I do?

I run Kesper Design, a sports graphics business where I create commitment edits, design branding for teams and clubs, and sell digital templates that athletes actually want to use. My Etsy shop has hit bestseller lists, and I've worked with hundreds of athletes nationwide.

This past summer, I was a Tech Intern at ComcastNBCUniversal -- developing an AI app in Flutter, prototyping with Figma, and learning how product design, dev, and strategy all come together. I'm interested in roles at the intersection of tech, storytelling, and user impact.
