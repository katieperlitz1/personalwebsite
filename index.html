ABOUT ME

SPORTS & FITNESS

Athletics, design for teams, Hyrox

I've always been into sports and fitness — whether I'm playing them, watching them, or designing for teams. I was a three-sport varsity athlete in high school (soccer, basketball, and lacrosse) and still stay active through running, lifting, and playing club basketball and lacrosse at Cornell.

College basketball is hands-down my favorite to watch, and I love capturing the energy and hype of big moments through my graphics and videos. Lately, I've been training for Hyrox races. I competed in my first in Summer 2025 and am gearing up for my next one in 2026.

BUSINESS & ENTREPRENEURSHIP

Startups, podcasts, TalentTap Cornell

I love learning about businesses as much as I love creating them. In my free time you'll find me reading business books and listening to podcasts, exploring investing trends, and obsessing over new AI tools released every week.

I've tried a new business almost every year since middle school (eBay, Shopify, selling ads on my viral Instagram page), and every project taught me something about building, failing, and iterating. I'm also a member of Epsilon Nu Tau, Cornell's Entrepreneurship fraternity, and am building TalentTap Cornell this Fall!

FILM & MUSIC

Video editing, After Effects, EDM

Two newer hobbies of mine are video editing and music production, and I've especially enjoyed editing movie scenes and sports clips together to create videos. I'm currently learning Adobe After Effects to improve my videos and exploring EDM/House music production.

I always appreciate a great film, and especially love thriller or plot-twist movies. Some of my favorites are Knives Out, Interstellar, Catch Me If You Can, and Parasite. Whenever anyone asks what I'd do if money were no object, I say I'd work on movie trailers or music videos.
