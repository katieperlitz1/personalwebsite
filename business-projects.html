Business and Creative Work

Kesper Design Committed Edits

2019-Present

Since 2019 I've been running my own graphic design business primarily through my Instagram page, @kesperdesign. I've created over 500 designs for high school athletes committing to college, graphics for sports teams and programs, and logos and YouTube thumbnails.

Etsy Store

Spring 2023-Present

I design and sell Canva and Photoshop templates on Etsy for teams to use as social media graphics. These include schedules, player spotlights, Gameday announcements, tryouts/event flyers, and other necessary promotional posts. Since starting my store in Spring 2023, I've become a top seller in the sports graphics niche and have generated over $5200 in revenue. I'm looking to expand and scale my store with more products in the next few months!
